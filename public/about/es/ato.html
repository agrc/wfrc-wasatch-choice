A key strategy of the Wasatch Choice Vision is linking economic development, transportation, and housing decisions.

Access to Opportunities (ATO) is a measure of how well our land use and transportation system works together to connect people with the places they want to get to, such as workplaces. You can view Workplace ATO -- for either automobile or transit modes -- using the map's filter controls. These maps provide a view for how well Wasatch Front household and workplace locations can be connected within typical commutes. This dataset is also available from data.wfrc.org.

Equity Focus Areas identify populations that could benefit most from increased ATO.
