Population and Employment Projections
Utah's population continues to grow. Internal growth, from our own kids and grandkids, continues to be the largest source of growth. To successfully plan for our region's future, we all must have a good idea of where people will live and work in the future.

The Wasatch Front Real Estate Market Model (REMM), developed by WFRC and MAG using UrbanSim software, helps to better understand current and future locations of households and jobs. REMM forecasts the population and employment distribution within each county to small area traffic analysis zone (TAZ) geography. The overall county totals are developed by the University of Utah's Gardner Policy Institute.

You can use the map's filter controls to view the projected distribution of population and jobs in 2050, or download this data from data.wfrc.org.
