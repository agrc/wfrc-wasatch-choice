Transportation choices is a Wasatch Choice Vision key strategy for a thriving region and communities.

Transportation Choices include driving, public transportation, and active transportation.

Major transportation projects, like freeways, rail & bus transit, interchange upgrades, widenings, and regional trails or bikeways get their start through inclusion in the Regional Transportation Plan (RTP). RTPs are updated every four years and plan 20 or more years into the future. All of Utah's RTPs combine into Utah's Unified Transportation Plan.

This map shows projects in the most recent 2023-50 RTPs that were adopted by the respective boards of elected officials for the Wasatch Front Regional Council (WFRC) and  Mountainland Association of Governments (MAG) metropolitan areas, in consultation with UDOT, UTA, cities and counties, and after extensive stakeholder and public input.

Prior to adoption, the projects were prioritized after considering technical analysis of impacts and benefits and extensive stakeholder and public input.
