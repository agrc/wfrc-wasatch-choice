HB462: Station Area Planning

Implementing the Wasatch Choice Vision
to preserve and enhance quality of life
as Utah grows

HB462 (2022) seeks to maximize development potential, and thereby increase the availability and affordability of housing, around transit stations -- almost all of which are within Wasatch Choice "Centers." Cities will develop Station Area Plans within 1/2 mile of rail transit stations and 1/4 mile of BRT stations with dedicated lanes, as listed below.
